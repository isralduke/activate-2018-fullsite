Activate is an event that aims to enrich our local tech community in Baton Rouge, Louisiana by establishing and furthering the careers of individuals in the web & technology field.

Our goal is to bring awareness of this field to the young community and build bridges between the future and current workforce in order to increase interest in careers in technology. We also invite current professionals to participate in furthering their skills to build a stronger workforce.
